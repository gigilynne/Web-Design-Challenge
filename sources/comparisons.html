Comparisons, Latitude vs X

After a full review of the data and the various plots, I have concluded that it is not only hotter as you move towards the equator, it is also more humid. Cloudiness and windspeeds south of the equator are less easy to predict but it is clear that as you reach either the North or South Pole, you will find extreme cold, wind, and lack of clouds. Ultimately, I decided I would highly recommend taking sunblock to the equator, and not only warm gloves, but a nice wind breaker to the poles.
