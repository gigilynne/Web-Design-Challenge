Latitiude vs Wind Speed

Wind speeds appear to be fairly consistent across the majority of the latitudes, with the exception of the furthest extremes both north, and south of the equator. Both of the extremes register either zero, or outlier wind speeds. The strongest winds appear in some of the northernmost distances from the equator.

As with all of the plots, there is a clear correlation to the latitude and the impact to the weather.
